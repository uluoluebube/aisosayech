About the company

Building reliable power for a smarter Nigeria.

Aisosa Technology is a Benin City based technology company focused on clean energy systems today, and a broader future of EV charging, smart gadgets and practical technology infrastructure tomorrow.

Our belief

Sustainability is a Mindset

Power should not feel like stress. For too long, homes and businesses have had to build their lives around unstable supply, generator noise and fuel costs. Aisosa Technology exists to change that with systems that are properly assessed, professionally installed and easy to maintain.

Premium, not complicated

We explain what each client needs in plain language, then design a system that matches their load, budget and future growth.

Engineering-led decisions

We size panels, inverters and batteries around usage patterns instead of guessing with generic packages.

Future-facing technology

Solar is the entry point. The long-term vision includes EV readiness, smart devices and broader technology solutions.

How we work

Assess. Design. Install. Support.

01 · Site Assessment

We inspect the location, calculate daily energy demand and identify the right panel, inverter and battery requirements.

02 · System Design

We match the proposed system to your appliances, backup needs, space and budget.

03 · Professional Install

Our technicians deliver clean, standards-compliant installation with support after setup.
