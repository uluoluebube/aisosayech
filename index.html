Who We Are

Built on a
conviction.

Aisosa Technology was born in Benin City out of a simple but urgent belief: Nigeria deserves better energy. Not excuses. Not apologies. Not another generator running on expensive, polluting fuel. Real, clean, permanent power.

We started with solar because solar made sense — technically, economically, and environmentally. We've since powered homes, businesses, offices, and industrial facilities across Edo State, building a reputation for honest advice, quality equipment, and installations that genuinely last.

We are not just an energy company. We are a technology company with a mission — to build the infrastructure that powers a smarter, cleaner, more connected Nigeria.

FUTURE
Looking Ahead

Beyond
Solar.

Solar is where we started. But Aisosa Technology is building toward something far bigger. We're expanding into electric vehicles, smart gadgets, and the full ecosystem of clean technology — because sustainability is not one product. It's a way of life.

As Nigeria's energy and technology landscape evolves, we intend to be at the center of it — bringing world-class solutions to our community, creating jobs, and proving that Benin City can be a hub of technological innovation.
